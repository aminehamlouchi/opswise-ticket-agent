## Steps

- Verify the requester's identity using the approved internal process.
- Check whether the account is locked, disabled, expired, or missing group membership.
- Confirm MFA enrollment status and recent failed sign-in attempts.
- Reset credentials or update permissions only after approval is documented.

Escalation: Escalate to identity administration if privileged access, repeated lockouts, or missing approval is involved.
